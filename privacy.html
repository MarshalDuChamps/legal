Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Refined Vandal Auto-Poster ("the App") handles data. The App is a personal content automation tool operated by Refined Vandal, a creative brand based in Hamburg, Germany. It is used exclusively to publish original content to TikTok.

Data We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or process personal data from TikTok users or any third parties. The only data accessed is:

  • The brand owner's TikTok account credentials (via OAuth 2.0 authorization)
  • Video and image files created by the brand owner for publishing
  • Post metadata (captions, hashtags, privacy settings) defined by the brand owner

How We Use Data

Data is used solely to publish original content from the brand owner's content queue to their TikTok account. No data is used for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any other purpose.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. The App interacts with the following services exclusively for content publishing:

  • TikTok Content Posting API — to publish videos
  • Make.com — workflow automation platform
  • Cloudinary — media file hosting

Data Storage & Security

OAuth tokens are stored securely within the Make.com platform. No personal data is stored on separate servers or databases. Media files are hosted temporarily on Cloudinary and are managed by the brand owner.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from minors.

Your Rights (GDPR)

As the App does not collect personal data from third parties, there is no user data to access, modify, or delete. If you believe your data has been processed in error, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
